About Me

And my journey to creating this page.

I'm Delphine, I come from France and have moved to Edinburgh in 2010.

2017 is a year that marked a change for me. I used to think my career was the most important thing. After some issues with my mental health, I decided to focus on what made me happy to live a healthier and more fulfilled life.

I spent more time travelling especially sailing around Scotland. I changed career to have a better work life balance.

These blogs are about my journey, my passions and my travels. How keeping true to myself has led me to live a happier life.
